How to Use

Why use this app?

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It's lightweight, fast, and works offline once loaded. No app store download needed!

Add to Home Screen

iPhone (Safari):
Tap the Share button (box with arrow) → Scroll down → Tap "Add to Home Screen".

Android (Chrome):
Tap the three dots (menu) → Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install App".

Favorites

Tap the ★ next to any artist to save them. Use the toggle at the top to see only your favorites. The stage color (e.g., Green for Arena) is preserved!
